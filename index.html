FAQ

What makes your toothpaste eco-friendly?

Our toothpaste is packaged in biodegradable tubes that break down in landfills within 6–10 years, unlike traditional tubes that can stay in the environment for over 500 years. We are the first in Australia to introduce this innovation.

What ingredients are in your toothpaste?

Our toothpaste is made from natural ingredients like mint, coconut oil, and green tea. It also contains essential vitamins and minerals for whole mouth health, such as vitamin B12.

Is your toothpaste suitable for vegans?

Yes, our toothpaste is 100% vegan and contains no animal-derived ingredients. We are also proud to say it is cruelty-free, meaning it is not tested on animals.

How does your toothpaste help with oral health?

Our toothpaste contains natural components that help fight cavities, freshen breath, and promote healthy gums. For example, the vitamin B12 in our toothpaste helps vegans maintain a normal level of this important vitamin.

Does your toothpaste contain harsh chemicals or artificial flavors?

No, we don’t use harmful chemicals or extreme flavors like "Arctic Blast." Our product contains only natural and safe ingredients that care for your oral health.

Can I use your toothpaste if I have sensitive teeth?

Yes, our products are designed to be gentle and safe, making them suitable for people with sensitive teeth. We use only natural ingredients that won’t irritate your teeth or gums.
